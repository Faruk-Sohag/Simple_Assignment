Bangamata Sheikh Fojilatunnesa Mujib Science & Technology University (BSFMSTU)

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Bangamata Sheikh Fojilatunnesa Mujib Science & Technology University (BSFMSTU)

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, the first department of its kind, was established in 2018 under the Faculty of Engineering. From the very initial days of its establishment, it has been able to attract the very best students. Students securing topmost merit positions in the admission test of BSFMSTU opt for studies in this department

At present, the department offered only B.Sc. Engineering Degree in Computer Science and Engineering. The Undergraduate program started on 01 April 2019. Total 34 students are admitted each year for pursuing the B.Sc. Engineering degree.


Some Important Facts about Dept. of CSE, BSFMSTU

  1. Number of Teachers: 5
  2. Number of Students: 170
  3. Number of Laboratories: 06
  4. Number of Staffs: 03

The CSE department has six modern laboratories:

  • Programming Lab
  • Software Engineering Lab
  • Networking Lab
  • Analog and Digital Electronics Lab (Shared with EEE Dept.)
  • Circuit Analysis Lab (Shared with EEE Dept.)
  • Communication Lab (Shared with EEE Dept.)
